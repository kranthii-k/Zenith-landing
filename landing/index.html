System Architecture

Intelligence-Gated Escrow

Solving the core problems of fake data, collusion, and irreversible theft.

The Core Problem

Current systems track money, not truth. They are vulnerable to fake data, human collusion, and irreversible theft since audits happen after funds are spent.

Milestone-Based Locking

Funds act as a "digital immune system." Money is only released when absolute cryptographic proof of physical work in the real world is provided.

Multi-Role Ecosystem

A strict interaction loop between Donors (financiers), NGOs (executors), and Auditors (verification experts), ensuring total transparency.

Advanced Defenses

Off-chain threat monitoring scans external news/socials to pause funds proactively, while Soulbound NFTs build an immutable reputation for contractors.

Workflow

The 5-Step Fund Lifecycle

Every transaction goes through a strict, automated pipeline.

Step 1

Allocation

Donors or government entities deposit funds. The system uses Chainlink CCIP to allow global liquidity, routing cross-chain capital seamlessly.

Step 2

Claim

The NGO finishes a milestone. To request payment, they must upload Geotagged IPFS Imagery as immutable proof of the physical work.

Step 3

AI Scrutiny

Before humans see the claim, an off-chain AI Oracle scans the submission for fraud, price anomalies, or inconsistencies using ML and Grok signals.

Step 4

Dynamic Audit

If AI flags the claim, the smart contract shifts from 1 signature to a 2-of-3 Multi-Sig requirement, forcing multiple auditors to verify it.

Step 5

Reputation

Once verified, funds are released. The system mints an AuditSeal NFT—a Soulbound token that permanently adds to the contractor's Trust Score.
